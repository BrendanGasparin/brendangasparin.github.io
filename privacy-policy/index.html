Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 January 2025.

Brendan Gasparin (trading as GASPARINTECH) is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you interact with our website or services:

  • Name: To address you personally in communications.
  • Email Address: To respond to your enquiries or provide updates.
  • Phone Number: To contact you directly if required.

We collect this information when you:

  • Fill out a contact or inquiry form on our website.
  • Communicate with us directly via email, phone, or other channels.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide requested services.
  • To communicate updates, offers, or relevant information about our services.
  • To improve our website, services, and customer experience.

We will not use your personal information for purposes other than those outlined above unless required by law or with your specific consent.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We respect your privacy and will not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to:

  • Provide services you have requested.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to secure your personal information against loss, unauthorised access, or misuse. You information is stored securely on protected systems, and only authorised personnel have access to it.

5. Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and collect website usage data. These cookies do not store personally identifiable information. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with our Cookie Policy.
