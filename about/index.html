About Brendan Gasparin

A short biography of Brendan Gasparin.

Background

Hi! I'm Brendan Gasparin, a web developer and designer. I grew up and live in Hobart, Tasmania, Australia.

I was the weird kid in school who played too many video games and ran Dungeons & Dragons campaigns. Even as an '80s kid I was into computers. I learned BASIC from the manual that came with my Commodore 64. I would copy video game code from magazines, and programmed my own text-based RPGs.

In the mid-90s, I landed a job creating websites for a local web development agency. This was my introduction to the amazing world of the Internet. I would write copy and code for about thirty web pages a day, serving clients around Australia.

I went to university to learn more about computer science. Here I learned many programming languages, including advanced web development languages such as JavaScript and PHP, and SQL for database management.

After the pandemic I realised I had the skills necessary to become your favourite freelance web consultant. Now I am here to serve your Internet needs.

Skills and Expertise

With over twenty-five years experience in web technology, I specialise in:

  • Building fast, secure, and visually stunning websites.
  • Responsive designs that look amazing across multiple devices and screen sizes.
  • Tailoring your content to take full advantage of search engine optimization (SEO) so you can rank on Google.
  • Engaging user experiences.
  • Ensuring your website is optimized to load as quickly as possible.

My expertise spans HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, SQL, other languages and frameworks, and platforms such as WordPress and Webflow.

Don't know what those acronyms mean? Don't worry. You don't need to. It's my job to convert your ideas into the designs and code that bring them to life.

My dedication is to understanding your unique vision. I know small business owners juggle countless responsibilities: professional, personal, and family commitments. That's why I focus on delivering streamlined, affordable solutions that save you time and money while generating leads, sales, and advertising for your business.

My approach is personal, collaborative, and backed by a 12 month guarantee.

Personal Interests

Outside of coding and design, I'm passionate about learning and creativity.

When I'm not building websites I can be found reading or writing (check out my blog), sketching and painting, playing video games and Dungeons & Dragons, or exploring Tasmania's beautiful landscapes with my kids and my BFF, Buddy the Beagador.
